About Invicta Group

Avianna Infra LLP, established in October 2021, is a notable developer in Navi Mumbai's real estate sector, focusing on innovative designs and timely project delivery. Their flagship project, Avianna Invicta, is situated in Ulwe, a rapidly developing suburb. This residential development offers 1, 2, and 3 BHK apartments with carpet areas ranging from 344 to 822 sq ft, catering to diverse homeowner needs.

The project spans 0.92 acres and comprises two towers, each with 11 floors, totaling 159 units. Amenities include a gymnasium, power backup, children's play area, and a swimming pool, enhancing the living experience. Strategically located, Avianna Invicta provides residents with easy access to essential services, making it a preferred choice for home seekers in Navi Mumbai.
